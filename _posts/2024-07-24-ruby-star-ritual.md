<h3 style="text-align: center;">How to Perform the Ruby Star Ritual: A Practical Guide</h3>
<p>The <strong>Ruby Star Ritual</strong>, or Star Ruby, is a potent Thelemic ceremonial practice created by Aleister Crowley. It is a ritual of protection and invocation done to purify the practitioner&#8217;s energy and mental space, invoke protective forces, and foster spiritual growth. Think of it as a means to gather friends, rather than simply banish foes. This guide provides a step-by-step process for performing the ritual and outlines its benefits.</p>
<p><strong>1. Preparation</strong><br />&#8211; Find a Quiet Space: Choose a private area where you won&#8217;t be disturbed.<br />&#8211; Set Up Your Altar: Include symbolic items like a pentagram representation or a deity figure.<br />&#8211; Gather Tools: You&#8217;ll need a wand, ritual dagger (athame), water cup, censer with incense, and the ritual text.</p>
<p><strong>2. Grounding and Centering</strong><br />&#8211; Relax and Focus: Use deep breathing to ground yourself and release distractions.</p>
<p><strong>3. Performing the Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP)</strong><br />&#8211; Purify the Space: Trace pentagrams with your wand, recite sacred names, and visualize cleansing energies. This step sets a protective, purified environment for the main ritual.</p>
<p><strong>4. The Ruby Star Ritual Steps</strong><br />&#8211; Opening Gesture: Stand facing east, perform the Sign of the Enterer, and vibrate &#8220;Thelema!&#8221; Visualize projecting your will outward.<br />&#8211; Invocation: Recite the Opening Invocation, calling upon your chosen divine or spiritual guides.<br />&#8211; Main Ritual Actions:<br />&#8211; Assume a balanced stance and perform the Sign of the Enterer, visualizing a radiant ruby star.<br />&#8211; Trace pentagrams with the ritual dagger, vibrate sacred names, and move clockwise around the space, visualizing the dispelling of negative energies and invoking protective forces.<br />&#8211; Closing Gesture: Conclude with the Sign of Silence to seal the ritual&#8217;s energy.</p>
<p><strong>5. Grounding and Integration</strong><br />&#8211; Return Excess Energy: Ground yourself by placing hands on the ground and visualizing energy flowing into the Earth.<br />&#8211; Reflect: Meditate or journal on your experience, noting any insights or emotions.</p>
<p>What to Expect from the <strong>Ruby Star Ritual</strong></p>
<p>Igniting the Divine Spark: The ritual helps awaken your inner divinity, fostering self-empowerment and encouraging the expression of your true self.</p>
<p>Banishing Negativity: It aids in releasing limiting beliefs and negative energies, clearing the way for personal growth and the manifestation of your True Will.</p>
<p>Protection and Boundaries: Establishes energetic protection, helping you maintain focus and integrity in daily life.</p>
<p>Spiritual Connection: Connects you with higher spiritual forces, providing guidance and inspiration. Again, part of the goal is to gather friends, rather than simply banish foes.</p>
<p>Inner Balance: Promotes mental clarity and emotional resilience, aiding in balanced decision-making and self-awareness.</p>
<p>Amplified Intentions: Enhances the power of your intentions and manifestations, aligning your actions with universal energies.</p>
<p>By following these steps, the <strong>Ruby Star Ritual</strong> can be a transformative tool, aligning you with your true purpose and empowering you to navigate life&#8217;s challenges with clarity and authenticity.</p>
